About me

I am not afraid to color outside the lines. In fact, it is when the colors cross lines and blank spaces are explored that more wholesome images emerge.

I have attempted to learn inside and outside the classroom and followed what my mind and heart desire. Here’s a window into my varied pursuits.


The Writer

I took to pen and paper instinctively as a child - the power of a well-written story to capture a mind and stimulate thought is magnificent. So I’ve taken up a few storytelling missions and hope to continue forever:
-Screenwriting program at New York Film Academy (virtual program)
-Written spoken and free-verse poetry pieces
-Analyzed methods and nuances of storytelling in films, TV and musical theatre.


The Dancer

It’s how I meditate. My 10-year-long training in Indian classical dance has allowed me to explore diverse dance forms like hip hop, contemporary, Bollywood and freestyle.
-Winner of interschool group dance competitions
-Lead performer at both my high schools’ annual day celebrations
-Winner of a solo talent competition for Lavani (Maharashtrian dance)
-Bharatnatyam (Indian classical art form) - ‘Arangetram’ or graduation ceremony practice is underway
-Self-taught choreographer



The Musician

Learning a musical instrument and practicing it puts my head and heart in a nurturing zone
-Amateur guitarist - Two and a half years
-Piano - Passed Trinity Grade 2


The Leader

It has always been my goal to use my energy to positively influence others and collaborate to yield collective benefits and use my power to act as an effective bridge between the students and staff.
-Grade 7 - Prefect
-Grade 9 - Green House Captain (elected)
-Grade 10 - School Captain (The Orchid School, interview selection)
-Grade 11-12 - Head Girl (Indus International School, Pune, elected, interviewed)
-Student Panelist - Webinar on Leadership
-Leadership conferences (SIETAR World Café, Student Leadership Conference)



The Student

The Human Being

I am intensely aware of the less fortunate amongst us.
-Spearheaded an effort as campaign manager to bring a virtual crowdfunding initiative with The Alpha Urbane Project to our school, raising over $ 32,000
(INR 23 lakhs) for Habitat for Humanity, for Covid-19 relief across India and personally raised INR 66,500
-‘Read A Story’ - As a writing volunteer, wrote short stories for children aged 4-6, which were illustrated and published online for the underprivileged
-Visiting volunteer at the world-renowned SECMOL in Ladakh, India
-Volunteered at Ekya, a local youth organization in my city, as a content writer and researcher for their Spotify podcast and a book on mental health awareness
